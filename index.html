The Center for Integrative Behavioral Medicine

The Center for Integrative Behavioral Medicine serves the Staten Island community with evaluation and referral services, mental health services, as well as substance abuse treatment and other specialized programs. Located at 1130 South Avenue in Staten Island, NY – on the grounds of the Hilton Garden Inn – our facility is easily accessible by public transportation, whether you’re coming from Brooklyn, Manhattan or Long Island, NY or Newark, NJ.
